I'm Kevin!


Born and raised in Fort Worth, TX, I earned degrees in Engineering Physics and Mathematics at the University of Oklahoma, and am a current M.S. Robotics candidate at Northeastern University in Boston, MA. I'm also working at Piaggio Fast Forward as a robotics software engineer! My expected final graduation is December 2023.

My primary areas of interest in robotics are state estimation and navigation, and I have experience using ROS and Kalman filtering to perform mobile robot localization. I'm also skilled in machine learning, and have specialized in evolutionary computation for my largest research projects. I'm actively working to deepen and expand my skillset to more areas of robotics software, including EKF-SLAM, trajectory planning, and model predictive control.

My non-technical hobbies include playing soccer, tennis, and dungeons & dragons. I'm an artist and enjoy penwork and digital painting. I'm also passionate about helping people learn! I loved working as a campus tour guide during my time at OU, and have spent most of my life involved with STEM education projects. I try to make all my projects open source and available for folks to learn from, so if you have a question about anything I've done, just ask!
