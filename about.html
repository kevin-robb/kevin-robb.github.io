I'm Kevin!


Born and raised in Fort Worth, TX, I earned degrees in Engineering Physics and Mathematics at the University of Oklahoma, and am currently working on my master's in Robotics at Northeastern University in Boston, MA.

My main interests are robotics, autonomous navigation, and artificial intelligence. I'm seeking opportunities to apply my knowledge and software skills in these areas, as well as to expand my electrical and mechanical experience.

My non-technical hobbies include playing soccer, tennis, and dungeons & dragons. I'm an artist and enjoy penwork and digital painting. Lastly, I'm passionate about helping people learn! I loved working as a campus tour guide during my time at OU, and have spent most of my life involved with STEM education projects.

If you'd like to contact me, I can be best reached by email at kevin.robb@alumni.ou.edu.
